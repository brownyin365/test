Dynamic Tax




What is Dynamic Tax? Dynamic tax minimizes or totally eliminates price manipulation by taxing buy and sale actions based on the current daily ROI (3.06%, 2.04%, 1.02% and 0.11%). .




Dynamic APY




What is APY? APY stands for Annual Percentage Yield. This measures the real rate of return on your principal tokens' amount by taking into account the effect of compounding interest. In the case of Classical Finance Protocol, your token represents your principal, and the compounded interest is added periodically on every Rebase event..
